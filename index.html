Getting Started with TradeSim Pro

Welcome to TradeSim Pro — a realistic paper trading simulator for crypto markets. We use live prices from Binance and professional charts from TradingView. Everything is free.

✅ Quick Start
  1. Create your free account (30 seconds)
  2. Go to Trade Terminal
  3. Select a pair (e.g. BTC/USDT)
  4. Set leverage (1x–100x)
  5. Optionally set TP/SL
  6. Click Buy or Sell
  7. Track positions in Dashboard · Get AI feedback

FAQ

Are the prices real? +
Yes. We fetch live prices from the Binance public API every few seconds. Your trades execute at real-time market prices. Only the money is simulated.
How does leverage work? +
Leverage multiplies your position size. With 10x leverage and ₹1,000 margin, you control a ₹10,000 position. Profits and losses are amplified by the leverage multiplier. With 100x leverage, a 1% move = 100% profit or loss on your margin.
How does the AI Coach work? +
The AI Coach uses Claude (Anthropic) to analyze your trading history. It calculates win rate, profit factor, risk per trade, detects patterns like overtrading, and gives personalized advice. You can also chat with it directly.
Is my data saved? +
Trades are stored in your browser's localStorage. In production, all data persists across devices with a cloud backend. Your data never leaves your device in this demo.
Is TradeSim Pro completely free? +
Yes! Everything on TradeSim Pro is free. All features including chart replay, strategy testing, AI Coach chat, competitions, and full 100x leverage simulation are available at no cost.
